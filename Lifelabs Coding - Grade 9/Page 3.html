Which Kind of Gaming-Related Content Attracts the Most Views?

There are many categories of gaming content on YouTube:

  • Funny Moments
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Tutorials
  • Professional Gameplay
  • Game Updates
  • And alot more...

Out of all these categories, there are 3 that are most common and most watched.
The category that gains the most views is "Funny Moments" which get up to
70 million views in a video. Followed by "Professional Gameplay" videos which can
get up 600 million views in a year. Next up are "Tips and Tricks" videos
which usually get 100,000 - 2.6 million views in a sigle upload.

Demographics

The main target audience of gaming content on YouTube are active gamers within the
age range of 12-25. This kind of content targets single teens and young adults who
actively play online, multiplayer games for 2-6 hours a day.


Profile of Viewers

There are over 3.1 billion people of all ages playing video games. And most of them are
within the age, of 12 to 24. That means that 40% of the world's population are watching
gaming videos and playing video games. Most of these gamers play online games actively
for 1-6 hours in day. These viewers are usually teenagers, single, and unemployed.


Viewers' Needs

Most viewers watching gaming content look for certain things in a video. We've done
some research and found out that these are what viewers are looking for in a video:

  • Tactics And Tutorials/lessons
  • How to improve
  • Non-toxicity
  • Fun and interesting gameplay
  • Game Updates
